About Scoops Ahoy

Our Sweet Story

Founded in 1985, Scoops Ahoy began as a small family-owned ice cream parlor with a simple mission: to bring joy to our community, one scoop at a time. What started as a humble shop with just six flavors has grown into a beloved local institution serving over 50 unique flavors of premium ice cream.

Our Philosophy

We believe that ice cream is more than just a dessert ‚Äì it's a moment of happiness, a celebration of life's sweet moments, and a way to bring people together. Every scoop is crafted with love, using only the finest ingredients sourced from local farms and premium suppliers.

Quality Promise

At Scoops Ahoy, we make all our ice cream fresh daily in small batches. We use real vanilla beans, fresh fruits, and premium chocolate to ensure every bite is perfect. Our commitment to quality means no artificial preservatives, no shortcuts, just pure delicious ice cream.

Community First

We're proud to be part of this amazing community. From sponsoring local sports teams to hosting birthday parties and community events, Scoops Ahoy is more than just an ice cream shop ‚Äì we're your neighbors, friends, and fellow ice cream enthusiasts.
